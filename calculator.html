How Scoring is Determined

The score of each Smasher is determined by two factors: popularity and placement. A Smasher can only earn points if he/she places in the top 64. Given that, the score of said Smasher increases with better placement and decreases with popularity. Thus, a tradeoff occurs that encourages people to choose underrated Smashers.
